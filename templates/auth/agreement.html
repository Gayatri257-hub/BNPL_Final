1. Introduction

This Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between you ("User", "Customer") and SmartPay Financial Technologies, a unit of PVG COEM, Pune, India ("SmartPay", "Company"). By using the SmartPay BNPL service, you acknowledge having read, understood, and agreed to the terms set forth in this Agreement. This Agreement is legally binding under the Information Technology Act, 2000, and applicable Indian financial regulations.

SmartPay provides an AI-powered credit evaluation and deferred payment service enabling eligible customers to purchase goods and services and repay the amount in structured Equal Monthly Instalments (EMIs) over the selected tenure.

2. Credit Assessment Consent

By proceeding with this Agreement, you explicitly consent to SmartPay collecting and processing your financial, behavioral, and identity data for the purpose of credit assessment. This includes, but is not limited to:

a) Transaction history and spending patterns on the SmartPay platform;
b) Device information including browser type, operating system, and IP address;
c) Geolocation data captured at the time of credit application;
d) KYC documents including PAN card information, Aadhaar linkage, and biometric liveness verification;
e) Behavioral signals such as session duration, interaction patterns, and time-of-activity.

Our AI models — including Logistic Regression for credit scoring, Isolation Forest for anomaly detection, and Random Forest for fraud classification — process this data to generate your Credit Score (300–900) and associated risk metrics. No manual underwriting is involved in the decision-making process.

3. Payment Obligations

Upon activation of a BNPL plan, you commit to paying the specified EMI amount on or before the due date each month. The EMI amount is computed based on the approved credit limit, selected tenure (3, 6, or 9 months), applicable interest rate (0% for scores ≥750, tiered for lower scores), and a one-time processing fee.

Payments must be made through the SmartPay portal. You are responsible for ensuring sufficient funds in your linked payment method. SmartPay reserves the right to retry failed payment mandates up to three (3) times within a grace period of 48 hours from the due date.

4. Late Payment & Penalties

A grace period of seven (7) calendar days from the EMI due date is provided. After this period, the following penalties apply:

• Days 8–30: Late fee of ₹150 or 2% of outstanding EMI amount, whichever is higher.
• Days 31–60: Additional penalty of ₹500 and suspension of further BNPL purchases.
• Days 61–90: Credit score degradation and account flagging in SmartPay's internal risk system.
• Beyond 90 days: See Legal Action Clause below.

⚠️
Legal Action Clause: In case of default exceeding 90 days, SmartPay reserves the right to initiate legal proceedings against the defaulting customer under applicable civil and criminal provisions of Indian law, and report the default to authorised Credit Information Companies (CICs) including CIBIL, Equifax, CRIF High Mark, and Experian. This will adversely affect your credit profile and may impact future loan, credit card, and BNPL applications with any financial institution in India.

5. Data Usage & Privacy

SmartPay is committed to protecting your personal data. All personally identifiable information (PII) including name, email, and phone number is encrypted at rest using AES-256 (Fernet) encryption. Sensitive financial identifiers such as PAN numbers are stored only in encrypted form and never shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

Your data may be shared with: (a) credit bureaus as required by RBI regulations, (b) law enforcement agencies upon valid legal request, (c) fraud detection partners operating under strict data processing agreements.

SmartPay does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to marketers or advertisers. You may request data deletion subject to regulatory retention requirements (minimum 7 years for financial records under Indian accounting standards).

6. Digital Signature Validity

Your electronic signature captured on this page constitutes a legally valid and enforceable signature under the Information Technology Act, 2000 (Section 5, Section 10A) and the Indian Evidence Act (Section 65B). The signature image is stored securely with a timestamp and cryptographic hash for audit purposes.

By signing, you confirm that: (a) You are at least 18 years of age, (b) You have the legal capacity to enter into this Agreement, (c) All information provided during registration and KYC is accurate and truthful, (d) You have read and understood all clauses of this Agreement.
