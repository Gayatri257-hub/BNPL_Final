SmartPay uses a 3-layer AI pipeline to evaluate your creditworthiness:

Logistic Regression — Primary credit scoring model trained on 10,000+ synthetic profiles. Weighs income, repayment history, and KYC quality to generate the base credit score (300–900).

Isolation Forest — Unsupervised anomaly detection that identifies outlier behavioral patterns such as unusual purchase velocities, device mismatches, or location anomalies. Generates the fraud score.

Random Forest — 100-tree ensemble classifier used for final approval decision and late-payment risk prediction. Combines all sub-scores to produce the approval probability.
